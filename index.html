Seller Terms and Conditions

1. Responsibilities of Sellers

  • Manage inventory responsibly and ensure accurate stock levels.
  • Maintain fair and transparent transactions with customers.
  • Demonstrate honesty and professionalism in all interactions.

2. Handling & Security of Products

  • No stealing, misplacing, or consuming products without payment.
  • Ensure proper storage of all products.
  • Report missing or misplaced items immediately.

3. Strike System & Consequences

  • 1st Strike: Verbal warning.
  • 2nd Strike: Written warning.
  • 3rd Strike: Deduction from earnings or temporary suspension.
  • 4th Strike: One-month suspension.
  • 5th Strike: Permanent removal.

4. Product Ownership

All product is owned by the owner. Any unsold items must be returned.

5. Reporting & Accountability

  • Report sales, stock levels, and any issues accurately.
  • All transactions must be recorded properly.

6. Payment & Earnings

  • Sellers are paid based on commission or an agreed-upon wage.
  • Mismanagement of money will result in disciplinary action.

7. Pricing & Discounts

  • Sell items at listed prices unless authorized otherwise.
  • No unauthorized discounts or giveaways.

8. Conduct & Customer Relations

  • Maintain professionalism when dealing with customers.
  • Rude or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

9. Prohibited Activities

  • No personal business or unauthorized item sales.
  • Unauthorized use of company property is forbidden.
